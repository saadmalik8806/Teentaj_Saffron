A heritage as old as time itself, a quality that has outlived generations, built with trust and truth as the foundation of our family. Our pure saffron is more than a product, it is an experience of a lifetime, something to cherish forever. Born and bred in India, also available online for home delivery, and shipping worldwide. This is how far we’ve come -

  • The Beginning

  • 1965

    Launch of Teentaj Brand Saffron

    An idea that bloomed into a collective vision for a healthy India by ensuring that the finest quality of premium & handpicked Saffron is available to you.

  • 2007

    In-House Packaging & Processing

    Facilitating safe and efficient packaging through our fully integrated plant for processing the best kesar in India.

  • 2012

    India’s First Exclusive Showroom

    Another feather in our cap as we set open our exclusive showroom for premium saffron in MGF Metropolis Mall Gurgaon.

  • 2014

    NABL-Accredited In-House Lab

    This year marks another milestone in our journey as we launch our in-house laboratory where handpicked saffron is tested in each batch for quality before it leaves our premises.

  • 2017

    A Fully Automated Factory

    The inauguration of a new and sprawling 25,000sq. ft. fully automated factory in Neemrana for saffron processing to make the best quality saffron in India available to you.
